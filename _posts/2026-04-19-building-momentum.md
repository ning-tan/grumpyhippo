The first post proved the setup; this one is about habit. Publishing twice in one day is a small, deliberate choice — not because every day will look like this, but because starting is easier when you chain one win to another.

Today I am writing down what I shipped (even if “shipped” means a markdown file and a `git push`), what felt unclear, and what I want to try next. That list does not need to be long. Two or three honest bullets beat a polished roadmap I will ignore next week.

If you are building something quietly on the side, this is your reminder that a second step on day one counts. I will be back with more when there is something worth the scroll.
